# Evaluation Questions

## Category A – Easy Questions (Expected: Correct Answer, High Confidence)

1. What is force majeure?
2. What events qualify as force majeure?
3. What happens if force majeure continues beyond 30 days?
4. What is the termination clause for bankruptcy?
5. When can the Bank terminate the agreement without notice?
6. What happens after termination due to force majeure?
7. What must a party do when a force majeure event occurs?
8. Does the agreement require notice before normal termination?
9. What payments are due after termination?
10. What is the purpose of the employee handbook?

---

## Category B – Ambiguous Questions (Expected: Partial Answer, Medium Confidence)

1. Can termination occur immediately?
2. What happens after 60 days of force majeure?
3. Can confidential information ever be disclosed?
4. Is written notice always required before termination?
5. Can the agreement be terminated for poor performance?
6. Does force majeure excuse every contractual obligation?
7. Can the Bank terminate for any reason?
8. Does the agreement specify exact notice periods?
9. Can a contract continue after bankruptcy?
10. Are cure periods always required?

---

## Category C – Impossible Questions (Expected: Refusal, High Confidence)

1. Who is the CEO?
2. Who drafted the agreement?
3. What office approved this agreement?
4. What is the company's annual revenue?
5. Can an employee work another job?
6. Who signed the employee handbook?
7. What is the Service Provider's address?
8. What software version was used to create this agreement?
